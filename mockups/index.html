v2 — overlapping plans model

Plans are additive: a new delivery spawns a new plan alongside any in-progress ones. Pantry, shopping list, favourites and history are global and reconcile across plans. Plans are archived manually with a 1–5★ rating used to filter history and tune future suggestions.
