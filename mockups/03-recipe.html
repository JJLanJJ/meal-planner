Pan-Seared Steak with Smashed Potatoes

Rump cooked to medium-rare with a garlic-butter baste, crispy smashed potatoes and charred broccoli with chilli & lemon.

⏱ 30 min total 10 prep 20 cook 2 adults + 1 child Medium

Ingredients

Rump steak500g
Baby potatoes500g
Broccoli1 head
Butter50g
Garlic4 cloves
Olive oil, salt, pepperto taste
Lemon1
From delivery Pantry To buy

Method

1

5 min

Take the steak out of the fridge to come to room temperature. Heat oven to 220°C. Bring a large pot of salted water to the boil.

2

12 min

Boil the potatoes until tender (about 12 min). Drain, transfer to a baking tray, smash gently with the bottom of a glass, drizzle generously with olive oil and salt. Roast 15 min until crisp.

3

5 min

Cut the broccoli into florets. Toss with olive oil, salt, a pinch of chilli flakes, and add to the tray with the potatoes for the last 8 min of roasting.

For the child portion: skip the chilli flakes on her broccoli.
4

6 min

Pat steak dry, season heavily. Heat a heavy pan over high heat with a splash of oil. Sear steak 3 min per side. Add butter and crushed garlic, baste for 30 seconds, then rest on a board for 5 min.

5

2 min

Slice steak against the grain. Plate with potatoes and broccoli. Squeeze over lemon, spoon over the resting juices.

Child portion: ~120g steak, smaller potato & broccoli serve.
